Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

To start selling, you have to create an account on the Baricoin web or mobile applications.

Sign up with these easy steps:

  1. Register with your email and phone number
  2. Verify your email with the OTP sent to it
  3. Set your password and PIN (use a strong password you'll remember)
  4. Start selling!

Selling gift cards is simple:

  1. Log in to your Baricoin account
  2. Select "Giftcard" from the menu
  3. Choose the card type and currency
  4. Upload clear photos of your card
  5. Enter the card details and submit
  6. Wait for verification and receive payment instantly

Selling crypto on Baricoin is easy:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Select "Crypto" from the menu
  3. Choose the cryptocurrency you want to sell
  4. Enter the amount in USD
  5. Copy the wallet address provided
  6. Send crypto to the address
  7. Receive payment after confirmation

Payment times vary by transaction type:

  • Gift Cards: Usually within 5-15 minutes after verification
  • Cryptocurrency: After blockchain confirmation (typically 10-30 minutes)
  • Withdrawals: Instant to your bank account

We support direct bank transfers to all Nigerian banks. Payments are made instantly to your registered bank account once your transaction is verified.
