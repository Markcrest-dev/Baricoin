1. Navigate to the **Support** page.
2. Use the **Search Bar** to find answers to your questions.

1. Scroll down to the "Submit a Support Ticket" form.

**Managing Notifications:**
1. Log in to your dashboard.
2. Click the **Notifications** link in the sidebar (or bell icon if available).
3. View your latest alerts.
4. Use the tabs to filter by type (Transactions, System, etc.).

3. Toggle Email, Push, SMS, and Sound preferences as desired.
