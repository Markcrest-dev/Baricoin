Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

This Privacy Policy describes how Baricoin Digital Technologies Limited collects, uses, and discloses information about users of The Baricoin app.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect personal information, such as your name and email address, when you register for an account or otherwise provide it to us.

Device Information: We may collect information about your device, such as its make and model, operating system version, and other technical information.

Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our app, such as the features you use and the actions you take.

Image Information: We may collect and use certain types of Image information from users of our app. This information may include images uploaded by users, metadata associated with those images, and other information related to the images.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our app and its features.
  • To personalize and customize the content and recommendations we provide to you.
  • To communicate with you about our app and other products and services that may be of interest to you.
  • To analyze and improve our app and its features, as well as to develop new products and services.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • To provide certain features and functionality of our app that require access to users' images.
  • To improve the quality and performance of our app by analyzing how users interact with our app and identifying areas for improvement.
  • To provide users with personalized content and recommendations based on their use of our app.

How We Disclose Information

Service Providers: We may disclose information to third-party service providers that assist us in providing our app or that help us to analyze and improve our app.

Business Partners: We may disclose information to our business partners, such as advertisers and sponsors, to enable them to provide targeted advertising and other promotional materials.

Legal and Regulatory Authorities: We may disclose information in response to a legal or regulatory request or if we believe that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the safety of our users.

Image Information: We may collect and use certain types of Image information from users of our app. This information may include images uploaded by users, metadata associated with those images, and other information related to the images.

User Choices

We provide users with the ability to control the collection and use of their information by allowing users to adjust their device settings or preferences within our app. However, please note that some features of our app may not be available if a user chooses to limit the collection or use of certain types of information.

Security

We take appropriate measures to safeguard the information we collect and to protect it against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards designed to protect your information and maintain data accuracy. We regularly review our security practices and update them as necessary to ensure the highest level of protection for your data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our systems promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting a new version on our website or within our app. Your continued use of our app after any such changes have been made constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@baricoin.com
